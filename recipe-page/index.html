Classic Pancakes

These classic pancakes are soft, fluffy, and easy to make using simple ingredients. They are perfect for a quick breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and egg, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and lightly grease it with butter.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
